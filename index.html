Introduction

The knight is the small insect protagonist of the indie Metroidvania, Hollow Knight, in which their goal is to stop an infection from spreading and corrupting the citizens of a kingdom of bugs called Hallownest. The reason I chose the Knight is because they are a character full of depth despite being completely silent. Their small statue makes enemies and bosses feel a lot larger and intimidating.
